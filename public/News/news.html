Falcons beat Bury to take the 2016 Northern League Title

It's taken 20 years but The Falcons finally took the Northern League Div. One Title.

Summed up nicely in a Facebook posting from George Swanson:-

It has been a privilege following the Falcons for the last 4 years and witnessing them lift:-

  • 2014 - The Northern KO Cup
  • 2014 - Division 2 Winners
  • 2015 - Division 2 Winners
  • 2014 - British Under 13 Champion - Kieran Swanson.
  • 2015 - British Veteran Champion - Lee Lawrence.
  • 2016 - The Northern League Division 1 title .
The spirit and will to win by the team this year is unmatched in any sport I have been involved in. Having to contend with the loss of heat leader Jason Burke early season , major injuries to Lee Lawrence and Neil Swanson which curtailed their involvement and the limited appearances of Polish star Daniel Ordon , each and every rider rose to the challenge and covered their missing teammates. It was a real squad victory! Maybe one day they will receive the recognition they deserve .
